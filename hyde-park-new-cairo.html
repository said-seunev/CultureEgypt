Hyde Park New Cairo is an upscale development situated in the heart of New Cairo. It offers a range of property types with sizes ranging from 217 to 1287 square meters. The project is being developed by  Hyde Park  with an estimated completion date of July 2026. The properties are being sold in a core and shell condition.

Hyde park new cairo prices 


The starting price for properties in Hyde Park New Cairo is 19,520,000 EGP, with a price per square meter of 69,617 EGP. The downpayment starts at 5% or 976,000 EGP, and there is a payment plan of 32 installments over 8 years. The monthly payment for a property in Hyde Park New Cairo starts at 193,167 EGP.

Amenities and Facilities: 

Hyde Park New Cairo offers a wide range of facilities and amenities for residents to enjoy. The project ensures the safety and comfort of residents with 24/7 security and concierge services to be one of the best compounds in egypt new cairo. 


Location: 


The location of Hyde Park New Cairo provides a perfect balance between accessibility and tranquility. It is easily accessible from major highways, allowing convenient connectivity to other parts of Cairo. The development is situated in a well-established and sought-after neighborhood, close to schools, universities, hospitals, and business districts. It is surrounded by shopping centers, restaurants, and entertainment venues, enhancing the convenience and appeal of the location.


Project Highlights: 


Hyde Park New Cairo offers a diverse range of residential options, including apartments, duplexes, penthouses, and villas. Each unit is meticulously designed with premium finishing and modern architecture that combines aesthetics with functionality. The development provides scenic views of landscaped parks, water features, and tree-lined streets, creating a picturesque ambiance. The community layout is well-planned, ensuring privacy, tranquility, and a sense of belonging.


Why Invest in Hyde Park New Cairo: 


Investing in Hyde Park New Cairo offers several compelling benefits. The project is located in a thriving and rapidly developing area of New Cairo, with strong demand for quality housing. The developer has a proven track record, ensuring high construction and delivery standards. Investors can expect potential rental income and capital appreciation due to the upscale amenities and strategic location.
