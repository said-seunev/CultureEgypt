Best cafes in new cairo 

nour

New Cairo is a rapidly developing area in Cairo, Egypt, known for its modern infrastructure and amenities. While the café scene in New Cairo continues to evolve, here are some popular and highly regarded cafes that you can consider visiting

cafes in new cairo 

 

Paul Bakery & Patisserie:

Located in Cairo Festival City Mall, Paul offers a cozy atmosphere and a wide range of delicious pastries, sandwiches, and coffee. It's a great spot for a quick bite or a leisurely brunch.

 

Greco's:

Greco café is known for its fresh and flavorful food, including sandwiches, salads, and desserts. They also serve a variety of coffee and tea options. The relaxed ambiance and friendly service make it a popular choice among locals.

Mori Sushi Café:

If you enjoy sushi and Japanese cuisine, Mori Sushi Café is worth a visit. Alongside their sushi offerings, they also have a cozy café area where you can enjoy a cup of coffee or tea.

The Batter Half & Co.:

This charming café is known for its delectable desserts, including cakes, pastries, and ice cream. They also serve a variety of coffee and tea options. The cozy and inviting atmosphere makes it a great place to unwind.

Beanos:

With multiple branches across Cairo, Beanos is a well-known café chain that offers a comfortable environment and a diverse menu. They serve a range of sandwiches, salads, and pastries, along with a wide selection of hot and cold beverages.

Costa Coffee:

A well-established international coffee chain, Costa Coffee has several branches in New Cairo. They serve a variety of coffees, teas, and snacks, and provide a relaxed setting for catching up with friends or getting some work done.

Crave:

Located in Rehab City, Crave is a trendy café known for its vibrant atmosphere and delicious food. They offer a diverse menu that includes breakfast options, sandwiches, salads, and desserts, along with a variety of beverages.

Coffee Corner:

Coffee Corner is a charming café known for its warm and inviting atmosphere. They have a wide range of coffee options, from classic to specialty brews, and their skilled baristas ensure a high-quality coffee experience. The café also serves delicious pastries, sandwiches, and salads, making it a great spot for a quick meal or a leisurely snack.

 

TBS - The Bakery Shop:

TBS is a well-known bakery and café in New Cairo. They offer a variety of freshly baked goods, including bread, pastries, cakes, and cookies. Their café area provides a comfortable setting to enjoy a cup of coffee or tea while indulging in their delightful treats.

Urban Station:

Urban Station is a trendy coworking space that also houses a café. It provides a modern and vibrant environment for remote workers, freelancers, and entrepreneurs. Alongside their coworking facilities, they have a café area where you can enjoy a range of coffees, teas, and light snacks while working or socializing.

Daily Roast:

Daily Roast is a specialty coffee shop that focuses on sourcing and roasting high-quality beans. They take pride in offering a selection of single-origin coffees and unique blends, prepared with precision and expertise. The café has a minimalist and contemporary design, creating a calm and cozy atmosphere for coffee enthusiasts.

Crumbs:

Crumbs is a bakery and café that specializes in artisanal bread and pastries. They have a wide array of baked goods, including croissants, baguettes, muffins, and cakes. The café area provides a cozy space to enjoy their freshly baked treats, accompanied by a cup of aromatic coffee or tea.

The Batter Half & Co. Roastery:

This establishment not only offers delectable desserts but also operates as a coffee roastery. They focus on sourcing high-quality coffee beans and roasting them in-house to create exceptional coffee blends. You can enjoy their freshly roasted coffee alongside their signature desserts and pastries.


Cup & Couch:

Cup & Couch is a charming café that offers a cozy and intimate atmosphere. They serve a range of coffee and tea options, including specialty brews and herbal infusions. The café also has a small selection of light bites and desserts, making it an ideal spot for a relaxed and peaceful break
