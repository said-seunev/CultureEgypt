Egypt is known for its rich culinary traditions, and there are numerous restaurants throughout the country that offer a variety of delicious dishes. Here are a few popular restaurant options in Egypt

Best restaurant in new cairo 

New Cairo, a rapidly growing suburb of Cairo, Egypt, offers a wide range of dining options. While opinions on the "best" restaurant can vary based on personal taste, here are a few highly regarded restaurants in New Cairo 

Kazlak:

 Kazlak is known for its inviting atmosphere, with both indoor and outdoor seating options. The restaurant often creates a lively ambience, especially during evenings when live music performances take place. The menu at Kazlak includes a variety of Lebanese mezze (appetizers), such as hummus, tabbouleh, and moutabbal, as well as grilled meats like shish taouk (marinated chicken skewers) and kafta (spiced ground meat). They also serve traditional Lebanese desserts and beverages.

Mori Sushi:

 Mori Sushi offers a sleek and contemporary dining environment. The restaurant's menu features an extensive selection of sushi rolls, including both traditional and innovative creations. They use fresh ingredients and provide options for both raw and cooked sushi. In addition to sushi, Mori Sushi also offers other Japanese dishes like tempura, teriyaki, and udon noodles.

The Batter Half & Co.:

This café and bakery has a cozy and inviting atmosphere, with comfortable seating and a warm interior. The menu at The Batter Half & Co. includes a range of breakfast options, such as eggs benedict, pancakes, and omelets. They also serve a variety of sandwiches, salads, and artisanal bread. The bakery section offers freshly baked pastries, cakes, and desserts, making it an ideal spot for a sweet treat or a leisurely brunch.

El Dokan:

 El Dokan offers an authentic Egyptian dining experience. The restaurant's interior is designed to resemble a traditional Egyptian house, complete with rustic decorations and traditional furnishings. The menu features Egyptian classics like grilled meats (such as mixed grills and kofta), stuffed pigeon (a popular delicacy), and traditional Egyptian dishes like molokhia (green leafy vegetable stew) and koshari (a hearty dish made of rice, lentils, pasta, and caramelized onions).

Casper & Gambini's:

 Casper & Gambini's is a stylish and modern restaurant with a relaxed atmosphere. The menu is diverse, with options ranging from salads and sandwiches to pastas, pizzas, and grilled meats. They also offer vegetarian and vegan choices. Casper & Gambini's is known for using high-quality ingredients to create flavorful and well-presented dishes. The restaurant's outdoor seating area is particularly popular, providing a pleasant setting for dining.
